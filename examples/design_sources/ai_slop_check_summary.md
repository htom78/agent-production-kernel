# AI Slop Check Summary

Source: codex/skills/ai-slop-check.md from
https://github.com/Trystan-SA/claude-design-system-prompt
License: MIT

Structured checks mapped into APK:

- Replace generic gradients with flat or subtle tokenized color.
- Remove decorative emoji unless the brand or state semantics require it.
- Avoid default rounded cards with decorative left borders.
- Trace colors and spacing to tokens.
- Prefer real assets or honest placeholders over weak custom illustrations.
